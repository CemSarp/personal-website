About Me

I’m Cem Sarp, a 21 year old Computer Engineering student at Sabancı University (3rd year) with a strong interest in web development, data analytics, and technology that creates real impact. Originally from İzmir, I’ve always been curious about how things work whether it's a line of code or the way people connect through digital tools.

Outside of academics, I care about community involvement and personal growth. Through Sabancı’s Civil Involvement Project, I had the opportunity to mentor primary school students from disadvantaged backgrounds. Leading sessions on creativity, communication, and children’s rights.

When I’m not working on a project or learning something new, I enjoy meeting with friends, playing basketball, watching new films and disturbing my 8 year old cat. She keeps things interesting and makes sure I never spend too long staring at a screen.

I’m always exploring ways to combine technical skills with meaningful purpose, and I enjoy collaborating on projects that challenge me to grow—both as a developer and as a person.
